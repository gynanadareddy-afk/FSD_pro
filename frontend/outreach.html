Outreach Overview

Making Technology Accessible to Everyone

The CSE Department is committed to bridging the gap between academia and society through comprehensive outreach programs. We believe in making technology education accessible to all segments of society and fostering a culture of innovation beyond campus boundaries.

Our outreach initiatives focus on school students, rural communities, women in technology, and underrepresented groups. Through workshops, training programs, and collaborative projects, we aim to create a technologically literate society.
