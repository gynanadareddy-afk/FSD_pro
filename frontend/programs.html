Academic Programs

Comprehensive Programs for Future Tech Leaders

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering

4-year undergraduate program focusing on fundamental and advanced computer science concepts.

  • Core Computer Science
  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Industry Internships

Dual Degree in Computer Science and Engineering

5-year integrated program combining undergraduate and postgraduate studies in computer science.

  • Integrated B.Tech + M.Tech
  • Advanced Specialization
  • Research Opportunities

M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering

2-year postgraduate program with specialization in advanced computing technologies.

  • Advanced Algorithms
  • Research Projects
  • Industry Collaboration

M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (2024 Onwards)

2-year postgraduate program with updated curriculum and advanced computing technologies.

  • Updated Curriculum
  • Advanced Research
  • Industry Integration

M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Cyber Security

2-year specialized postgraduate program focusing on cybersecurity and information assurance.

  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  • Security Architecture
  • Ethical Hacking
